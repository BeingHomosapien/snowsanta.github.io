IIT MANDI's

About us

Nestled upon the crown of India, Himalayas, IIT Mandi is gearing up to present the 9th edition of its Annual Techno-cultural fest Exodia. Exodia with every progressing year is becoming culturally more vibrant and confluent technically. This year’s theme Tropical Getaway would let vastness of the sea set us free as well as acquaint us with the necessity of wandering without being lost. Aptly ensembled with Junkyard Wars, Ramp Walks, Art, Music, Code, Robots, Poems and Dance, we proudly present Exodia 2020.

Theme

“Surrounded by gigantic mountains, but yet the spirits of the sea.” The ocean stirs the heart, inspires the imagination and brings eternal joy to the soul. Mountains are a symbol of strength, persistance , tenacity and boldness. Whereas sea is a symbol of vastness, limitlessness and constant activity. This theme to provide its visitors the best of both worlds.
